About Me

Hello! I'm Hari Ragav S, an enthusiastic and driven Computer Science student currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sri City.

I have a strong interest in Full-Stack Development and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, with hands-on experience in building scalable applications and experimenting with intelligent systems. Over time, I’ve explored projects ranging from developing a Community Sports Management Platform (SportsAmigo), creating engaging portfolio websites, to designing interactive bubble-themed games in Unity for hackathons. I am passionate about solving real-world problems with technology—whether it’s through web technologies like Node.js, MongoDB, React, or AI/ML frameworks for computer vision and natural language processing. I thrive in dynamic team environments and am constantly seeking opportunities to learn, innovate, and create impactful solutions.
